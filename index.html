WELCOME TO MARTIN TRAVEL

With a rich history dating back to 1987, Martin Travel has been a trusted name in the travel industry. Our ability to adapt to the evolving landscape, from the era of handwritten tickets to the computer reservation system, showcases our commitment to staying ahead of the curve.

We have always embraced technology and the internet as a source of travel information. Our ability to operate our airline, car, hotel, and rail online system, Amadeus, has streamlined our operations, ensuring efficient and accurate bookings. Our cloud-based phone system, Nextiva, has improved our communication capabilities, allowing us to provide prompt and reliable customer service.
